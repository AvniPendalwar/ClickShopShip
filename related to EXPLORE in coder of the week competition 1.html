Click,Shop,Ship

Click,Shop,Ship is an online shopping platform which has a variety of clothes, toys, accessories, household items and lot more. People can purchase and sell items at a resonable rate. It also provides live- streaming videos and online payments. Special discounts are also available during a festive season. It also has a partnership with flipcart. It is now looking forward for home delivery of goods which not only includes clothes, toys, accessories etc, but also includes medicines and food items (fresh fruits/vegetables and ready to eat food). We took this decision by keeping in mind the circumstance and ensuring full safety. Our area is sanitized everyday, our employees wash their hands before cooking the meal, the food is nicely cooked and the ones which are raw are properly washed. Click,Shop,Ship was founded on 27 December, 2007 with it's headquarters in Chicago and Mumbai. In 2015 it became the most popular and used platform followed by amazon.
